Why Protein Binding Matters

Understanding the real-world impact of protein binder design

Blocking & Inhibition
Most Common Use: Stop harmful proteins from working

Examples:
  • • Cancer Treatment: Block growth signals to stop tumor growth
  • • COVID-19: Bind spike protein to prevent cell entry
  • • Autoimmune: Block inflammatory proteins to reduce symptoms

Example Drug: Humira blocks TNF-alpha ($20B+ annual sales)
Activation & Enhancement
Purpose: Make proteins work better or turn them on

Examples:
  • • Enzyme activation for metabolic disorders
  • • Stabilize therapeutic proteins
  • • Trigger beneficial signaling pathways
Delivery & Targeting
Purpose: Guide drugs to specific locations

Examples:
  • • Target cancer cells directly to reduce side effects
  • • Cross blood-brain barrier for neurological diseases
  • • Organ-specific drug delivery
Detection & Diagnostics
Purpose: Find and measure specific proteins

Examples:
  • • COVID rapid tests
  • • Cancer screening (tumor marker detection)
  • • Pregnancy tests (hCG detection)
Modification & Control
Purpose: Change how target proteins behave

Examples:
  • • Extend drug half-life (make medicines last longer)
  • • Control cellular localization
  • • Regulate protein-protein interactions
